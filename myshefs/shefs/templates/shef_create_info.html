How do orders work on Shef?

Here's how the process works:

1. Choose Your Availability

You choose the dates you are available to cook.

2. Customers Place Orders

Customers place their orders.

3. Cook, Cool Down, and Package

You cook, cool down, and package your food.

4. Bring Orders to Drop-Off Location

You bring your orders to the Shef drop-off location in the morning.

5. Shef Delivers to Customers

Shef will deliver to your customers in the afternoon.

6. Receive Payment

You will receive a direct deposit within a few days! Payments are processed 1 day after drop off.
